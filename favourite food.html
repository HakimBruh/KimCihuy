Fav Food

My favourite food is chicken tenders. They are crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, and always full of flavor. I love the golden-brown crunch when I take a bite, especially when they're freshly fried. Whether dipped in spicy sauce, honey mustard, or eaten plain, chicken tenders always make a perfect meal or snack. They're simple, delicious, and remind me of comfort and happiness every time I eat them.
